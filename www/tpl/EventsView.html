pause and resume

Triggered respectively when the application is put in the background and comes back to the foreground.
document.addEventListener("pause", onPause, false);
document.addEventListener("resume", onResume, false);

online and offline

Triggered when the app connectivity status changes to online or offline.
document.addEventListener("online", onOnline, false);
document.addEventListener("offline", onOffline, false);

battery

Triggered when the device battery status changes.
document.addEventListener("batterystatus", onBatteryStatus, false);
document.addEventListener("batterylow", onBatteryLow, false);
document.addEventListener("batterycritical", onBatteryCritical, false);

startcallbutton and endcallbutton

Triggered respectively whe the device starts and ends a call.
document.addEventListener("startcallbutton", onStartCall, false);
document.addEventListener("endcallbutton", onEndCall, false);
